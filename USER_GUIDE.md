# RemZy User Guide

## Quick Start Guide

### For Patients

1. **Create Your Account**
   - Open RemZy in your web browser
   - Click "Sign Up" on the login page
   - Choose a username and password (at least 6 characters)
   - Click "Sign Up"

2. **Select Patient Mode**
   - After signing in, you'll see two options
   - Click "Select Patient Mode"
   - This will lock your device to patient mode for security

3. **Complete Setup**
   - Enter your full name
   - Optionally add your date of birth
   - Optionally set your safe area location (home address)
   - Review your information and click "Complete Setup"

4. **Share Your Linking Code**
   - You'll receive a QR code and an 8-character linking code
   - Share this with your caregiver so they can connect to your device
   - You can find this code later in Settings

5. **Use Your Dashboard**
   - **AI Companion**: Chat with your AI assistant for help remembering things
   - **My Tasks**: View your daily tasks and reminders
   - **My Contacts**: See people you know
   - **Health**: Check your health information
   - **Emergency Help**: Large red button for emergencies

### For Caregivers

1. **Create Your Account**
   - Open RemZy in your web browser
   - Click "Sign Up" on the login page
   - Choose a username and password
   - Click "Sign Up"

2. **Select Caregiver Mode**
   - After signing in, you'll see two options
   - Click "Select Caregiver Mode"
   - This will lock your device to caregiver mode

3. **Complete Setup**
   - Enter your full name
   - Enter the 8-character linking code from your patient's device
   - Click "Complete Setup"

4. **Monitor Your Patients**
   - View all linked patients on your dashboard
   - See recent alerts and notifications
   - Access activity logs and health reports
   - Receive instant alerts for important events

## Features Explained

### Patient Features

#### AI Companion
- Ask questions like "What day is it?" or "Who am I?"
- Get help remembering your schedule
- Receive orientation assistance
- Chat naturally with the AI

#### Tasks
- View upcoming tasks and reminders
- Mark tasks as completed or skipped
- Add new tasks with times and locations
- Receive notifications for scheduled tasks

#### Contacts (Known Faces)
- Save information about people you know
- Add names, relationships, and notes
- View when you last saw each person
- Get reminders when you meet them

#### Health Monitoring
- Track heart rate and activity
- View step count and movement
- Monitor for abnormal patterns
- Share data with caregivers

#### Emergency Button
- Always visible on your dashboard
- One tap to alert all caregivers
- Sends your location automatically
- Provides immediate assistance

### Caregiver Features

#### Dashboard
- See all linked patients at a glance
- View unread alerts count
- Access quick actions
- Monitor patient status

#### Alerts
- Emergency notifications
- Task reminders (skipped or missed)
- Unknown person encounters
- Health abnormalities
- Safe area breaches

#### Patient Management
- Link multiple patient devices
- View patient information
- Access patient activity history
- Monitor health metrics

#### Activity Logs
- Complete history of patient activities
- Filter by date and type
- Search for specific events
- Export reports

#### Health Reports
- View health trends over time
- Monitor vital signs
- Track activity levels
- Identify patterns

## Tips for Best Use

### For Patients
- Keep your device charged and with you
- Use the AI Companion whenever you feel confused
- Don't hesitate to press the Emergency button if needed
- Review your tasks each morning
- Add people to your contacts as you meet them

### For Caregivers
- Check alerts regularly
- Review activity logs daily
- Keep your linking code secure
- Respond promptly to emergency alerts
- Update patient information as needed

## Privacy and Security

- All data is encrypted and secure
- Only linked caregivers can see patient data
- Device modes are locked after setup
- Linking codes are unique and secure
- No data is shared publicly

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Sign In
- Check your username and password
- Username is case-sensitive
- Password must be at least 6 characters

### Lost Linking Code
- Patients: Go to Settings to view your code
- Caregivers: Ask your patient to check their Settings

### Not Receiving Alerts
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure devices are properly linked
- Verify alert settings in Settings

### Device Mode Locked
- This is intentional for security
- Contact support if you need to change modes
- Create a new account if needed
